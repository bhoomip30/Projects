COFFEE HOUSE - RECEIPES


COLD BREW COFFEE

To make cold brew coffee:

1. Coarsely grind 1 cup of coffee beans.
2. Combine the coffee grounds with 4 cups of cold water in a large container.
3. Stir gently and let it steep at room temperature for 12 to 24 hours.
4. Strain the coffee to remove the grounds.
5. Transfer the cold brew concentrate to a clean container and store it in the refrigerator.
6. To serve, dilute the concentrate with water, milk, or your preferred liquid.
7. Add ice cubes, sweeteners, or flavorings if desired.
8. Stir well and enjoy!




MAGIC WHIPPED COFFEE CREAM

To make magic whipped coffee cream, also known as Dalgona coffee:

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water.
2. Using an electric mixer or whisk, beat the mixture on high speed until it becomes thick, creamy, and light in color. This can take about 2-5 minutes.
3. Fill a glass with milk and add ice cubes if desired.
4. Spoon the whipped coffee cream over the milk, filling the glass.
5. Optional: Top with additional whipped cream or sprinkle cocoa powder on top.
6. Stir the coffee cream into the milk before drinking.
7. Enjoy your magical whipped coffee cream!




TAHINI CHOCOLATE COFFEE DATE SHAKE

Here's a recipe for Tahini Chocolate Coffee Date Shake:

1. Place the frozen bananas, tahini, cocoa powder, brewed coffee, pitted dates, and milk in a blender.
2. Blend on high speed until all the ingredients are well combined and the shake is smooth and creamy.
3. If desired, add a few ice cubes to make the shake thicker and colder.
4. Taste and adjust the sweetness by adding more dates if needed.
5. Blend again until everything is well incorporated.
6. Pour the Tahini Chocolate Coffee Date Shake into glasses and serve immediately.
7. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious shake!




DREAMY LATTE

Here's a recipe for a dreamy latte:

1. Brew a shot of espresso or make strong coffee using your preferred method.
2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it starts to steam. Be careful not to boil the milk.
3. While the milk is heating, froth it using a milk frother, whisk, or blender until it becomes frothy and creamy.
4. Pour the hot espresso or coffee into a mug.
5. Add your desired sweetener and any optional flavorings to the coffee, stirring until the sweetener is dissolved.
6. Gently pour the frothed milk over the coffee, holding back the foam with a spoon if desired.
7. Spoon the remaining foam on top of the latte.
8. Optional: Sprinkle with a pinch of cocoa powder, cinnamon, or nutmeg for added flavor and presentation.
9. Enjoy your dreamy latte while it's still hot!




BISCOFF CLOUD COFFEE

Here's the recipe Biscoff Cloud Coffee:

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the Biscoff spread, sugar, and hot water.
2. Using an electric mixer or whisk, beat the mixture on high speed until it becomes thick, creamy, and light in color. This can take about 2-5 minutes.
3. Fill a glass with milk and add ice cubes if desired.
4. Prepare a cup of instant coffee or espresso according to your preference and pour it over the milk.
5. Spoon the Biscoff cloud cream over the coffee-milk mixture, filling the glass.
6. Optional: Top with whipped cream and crumble Biscoff cookies on top for extra flavor and decoration.
7. Stir the Biscoff cloud cream into the coffee before drinking.
8. Enjoy your delicious Biscoff Cloud Coffee!




VANILLA BEAN ICED COFFEE

To make vanilla bean iced coffee:

1. Brew a cup of coffee using your preferred method (e.g., drip, French press, or espresso). Let it cool down and chill in the refrigerator.
2. In a glass, combine the chilled coffee, milk, and vanilla bean syrup.
3. Stir the mixture well until the syrup is evenly distributed.
4. Add ice cubes to the glass, filling it to the top.
5. Stir again to chill the coffee further and ensure the flavors are well combined.
6. Taste the coffee and adjust the sweetness or strength according to your preference. You can add more vanilla bean syrup if desired.
7. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing vanilla bean iced coffee!
